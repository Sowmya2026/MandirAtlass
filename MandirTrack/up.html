1.🔱 Kashi Vishwanath Temple

📍 Temple Introduction

  • 📍 Location: Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh
  • 🕉️ Deity: Lord Shiva
  • 🏛️ Architecture: Nagara-style
  • 📅 Best Time to Visit: October – March

🕯️ Rituals & Festivals

  • Rituals & Festivals: Daily Pujas & Aarti Timings
  • Major Festivals: Mahashivratri, Kartik Purnima

🚶 Visitor Information & Tips

  • ⏰ Timings: 4:00 AM – 10:00 PM
  • 🎟 Entry Fee: Free (VIP Darshan ₹500)
  • 👗 Dress Code: Traditional attire preferred

🗺️ Nearby Attractions

  • 🛣️ 1 km → Dashashwamedh Ghat
  • 🛣️ 1.2 km → Kedar Ghat
  • 🛣️ 1.5 km → Manikarnika Ghat
  • 🛣️ 7 km → Banaras Hindu University
  • 🛣️ 10 km → Sarnath Buddhist Stupa
  • 🛣️ 14 km → Ramnagar Fort

📜 History & Legends

The Kashi Vishwanath Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, is one of the most significant temples in India. Located in the heart of Varanasi, it is one of the 12 Jyotirlingas, and has a deep spiritual significance for Hindus.
