1.🔱 Sri Jagannath Temple

📍 Temple Introduction

  • 📍 Location: Banjara Hills, Hyderabad, Telangana
  • 🕉️ Deity: Lord Jagannath
  • 🏛️ Architecture: Modern Hindu temple architecture
  • 📅 Best Time to Visit: October – March

🕯️ Rituals & Festivals

  • Rituals & Festivals: Daily Pujas & Aarti Timings
  • Major Festivals: Rath Yatra, Maha Shivaratri

🚶 Visitor Information & Tips

  • ⏰ Timings: 6:00 AM – 12:00 PM, 4:00 PM – 8:00 PM
  • 🎟 Entry Fee: Free
  • 👗 Dress Code: Traditional attire preferred

🗺️ Nearby Attractions

  • 🛣️ 2 km → Buddha Statue
  • 🛣️ 3 km → Hussain Sagar Lake
  • 🛣️ 5 km → Golconda Fort

📜 History & Legends

Sri Jagannath Temple in Hyderabad is dedicated to Lord Jagannath, built as a replica of the famous Puri Jagannath Temple in Odisha. The temple is known for its vibrant Rath Yatra festival, attracting devotees from across the region.

3.🔱 Srisailam Temple (Sri Mallikarjuna Swamy Temple)

📍 Temple Introduction

  • 📍 Location: Srisailam, Telangana
  • 🕉️ Deity: Lord Mallikarjuna (Shiva) & Goddess Bhramaramba (Parvati)
  • 🏛️ Architecture: Dravidian-style architecture
  • 📅 Best Time to Visit: October – March

🕯️ Rituals & Festivals

  • Rituals & Festivals: Daily Pujas & Aarti Timings
  • Major Festivals: Maha Shivaratri, Bhramaramba Kalyanam

🚶 Visitor Information & Tips

  • ⏰ Timings: 6:00 AM – 12:00 PM, 4:00 PM – 9:00 PM
  • 🎟 Entry Fee: Free
  • 👗 Dress Code: Traditional attire preferred

🗺️ Nearby Attractions

  • 🛣️ 5 km → Srisailam Dam
  • 🛣️ 8 km → Pathala Ganga
  • 🛣️ 12 km → Shikharam

📜 History & Legends

The Srisailam Temple is one of the 12 Jyotirlingas and is revered by Hindus as a sacred site. It is dedicated to Lord Mallikarjuna (Shiva) and Goddess Bhramaramba (Parvati). The temple is located amidst the beautiful Nallamala hills.

📍 Temple Introduction

  • 📍 Location: Bhadrachalam, Telangana
  • 🕉️ Deity: Lord Rama
  • 🏛️ Architecture: Kakatiya style architecture
  • 📅 Best Time to Visit: October – March

🕯️ Rituals & Festivals

  • Rituals & Festivals: Daily Pujas & Aarti Timings
  • Major Festivals: Rama Navami, Dussehra

🚶 Visitor Information & Tips

  • ⏰ Timings: 6:00 AM – 12:00 PM, 4:00 PM – 9:00 PM
  • 🎟 Entry Fee: Free
  • 👗 Dress Code: Traditional attire preferred

🗺️ Nearby Attractions

  • 🛣️ 2 km → Parnasala
  • 🛣️ 5 km → Kinnerasani Wildlife Sanctuary
  • 🛣️ 10 km → Papikondalu

📜 History & Legends

The Bhadrachalam Temple is dedicated to Lord Rama, where it is believed that Lord Rama and Sita stayed during their exile. The temple is a prominent pilgrimage site for devotees of Lord Rama and hosts a grand celebration of Rama Navami.
