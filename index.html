🌟 Welcome to MandirAtlas 🌟

🏛️ Aligned with SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities

MandirAtlas is your gateway to exploring temples across various regions, preserving cultural heritage, and engaging with a community passionate about temple history and traditions.

🌍 How MandirAtlas Supports SDG 11 🌍

1. 🕌 "Preserving Cultural Heritage:" The platform digitally documents temples, ensuring their historical and cultural significance is maintained for future generations.

2. 🚶‍♂️ "Promoting Sustainable Tourism:" By providing interactive temple maps and resources, MandirAtlas encourages responsible tourism that respects cultural sites.

3. 📍 "Enhancing Accessibility:" The website makes temple information easily available, allowing visitors to plan visits efficiently and reducing unnecessary travel.

4. 🤝 "Community Engagement:" Users can share experiences, participate in discussions, and contribute to temple data, fostering a strong cultural community.
